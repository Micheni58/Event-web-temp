Our Story

2018 — The Beginning

Founded with a passion for design and coordination, Exquisite started as a small home-based event planning idea, focused on making special days unforgettable.

2020 — Expanding Horizons

We began managing corporate and large-scale events, becoming known for our elegant themes and attention to detail.

2023 — Recognized Excellence

Awarded “Event Planner of the Year,” Exquisite gained recognition for creativity and professionalism across Kenya.

2025 — Beyond Boundaries

Today, we are a full-service event planning company trusted by individuals and brands to bring their visions to life in remarkable ways.
